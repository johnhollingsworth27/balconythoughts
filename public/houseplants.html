houseplants

Low Light Plants

Snake Plant (Sansevieria)

Watering: Allow the soil to dry out between waterings, approximately every 2-3 weeks.

ZZ Plant (Zamioculcas zamiifolia)

Watering: Water sparingly, allowing the soil to dry out completely between waterings, approximately every 2-4 weeks.

Pothos (Epipremnum aureum)

Watering: Allow the top inch of soil to dry out between waterings, approximately every 1-2 weeks.

Medium Light Plants

Rubber Plant (Ficus elastica)

Watering: Allow the top 1-2 inches of soil to dry out between waterings, approximately every 1-2 weeks.

Monstera (Monstera deliciosa)

Watering: Allow the top 1-2 inches of soil to dry out between waterings, approximately every 1-2 weeks.

Calathea (Calathea spp.)

Watering: Keep the soil consistently moist, watering when the top inch of soil is dry, approximately every 1-2 weeks.

High Light Plants

Fiddle Leaf Fig (Ficus lyrata)

Watering: Allow the top 1-2 inches of soil to dry out between waterings, approximately every 1-2 weeks.

Dracaena (Dracaena spp.)

Watering: Water moderately, allowing the top inch of soil to dry out between waterings, approximately every 1-2 weeks.

Aloe Vera

Watering: Water deeply, allowing the soil to dry out completely between waterings, approximately every 2-4 weeks.
